Mother Tree

Final Game Programing Project

Player begins the game next to the Mother Tree and can proceed to move around the pre-generated map using the keys: w to move up, a to move left, s to move down, d to move right. The player can also use an attack by pressing the spacebar. When an enemy dies, they drop a vile or blood that can then be collected by the player. The player has a bag with limited capacity where they can place the collected blood.

Fast forward is activated when the player enters the sleeping state. In this state, the game will continue to run in the background at a higher speed while the foreground shows a sleeping panel. This way, when the player wakes up, time would have passed realistically in the game.

All Enemies will have the same base mechanics but the amount of damage they deal, their speed and their health bar will be randomly set upon creation. Each non-player entity will have a basic AI that allows them to not only fight the player but each other as well. When an enemy kill another entity, their stats increase. Skeleton entities will have a random chance of spawning from the place where an enemy has died.

The day and night feature comprises of drawing a semi-transparent black box over the canvas to show the passage of time in the game.

Base code was take from Vikram Singh's (teachers) example code.
